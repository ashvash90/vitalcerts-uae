What is Attestation?

Attestation is the process of taking documents issued in one country and making them legally recognised in another. The process will vary from country to country, and by document type. How does it work?

Step 1

Send us your documents or have them collected by our specially allocated couriers...

Step 2

We'll have them sent safely to the country of origin, where they'll be legalised by the relevant government department and attested at the UAE Embassy

Step 3

They'll be returned to the UAE and attested at the Ministry of Foreign Affairs (MOFA), before being sent back to you (or anywhere else you might need), safe and sound
