Attestation, made simple

We're able to attest a range of documents for use in the UAE, no matter where you're from. You can rest assured that by using our service, your attested documents will be accepted within the UAE. These include (but are not limited to):

  • Birth, Marriage, Death & Adoption Certificates

  • Educational Qualifications

  • Identification Documents

  • Medical Documents

  • Court-Issued Documents

  • Financial & Tax Documents

  • Police Clearance Documents

  • Company Documents

And we can even arrange to have your documents collected from, and delivered to, anywhere within the UAE.

How does it work?

Step 1

Send us your documents or have them collected by our specially allocated couriers...

Step 2

We'll have them sent safely to the country of origin, where they'll be legalised by the relevant government department and attested at the UAE Embassy

Step 3

They'll be returned to the UAE and attested at the Ministry of Foreign Affairs (MOFA), before being sent back to you (or anywhere else you might need), safe and sound
