What's Important to Us?

We pride ourselves on the experience, knowledge and know-how we've gathered over the 16 years since we started trading. Here's the three things that we've most come to value over that time.

Acceptance Guaranteed

You can trust that any documents we attest from the country of issue are GUARANTEED to be accepted in your destination country. We also work closely with the Department of Economic Development where company documents require attestation for UAE company formations. You can view our Service Guarantee here.

Simple Professionalism

You won't find any flashy images on this site, or sensational claims. That's not our style. All we care about is providing the best possible service, with up-to-date information, so that you can relax and let us get on with the boring details so you don't have to.

A Personal Touch

Our clients come from every corner of the globe, and as such we have the experience and knowledge necessary to attest your documents no matter where you're from. Our specialist team based in Dubai will do everything it takes to make sure the attestation process goes smoothly, and that you can have your newly legalised documents delivered to you in the shortest time possible.
