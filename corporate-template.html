How can Vital Certificates help you?

Starting a company can be difficult enough, but setting up a business abroad can add even more bureaucracy and stress than you're used to! There are so many things you need to think about to be able to trade legally in the UAE. One of those is attestation.

Moving to the UAE? There are so many things to think about! One of those is attestation.

Attestation (sometimes known as verification or legalisation) is the process of taking a document issued in one country and making it legal for use in another. It's a vital process you'll require if you're thinking of living, working or starting a business in the GCC.

That's where Vital Certificates come in. We'll take care of the attestation for you so you can focus on the big stuff.
